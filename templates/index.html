Admission Requirements

National Diploma (ND)

  • Minimum of 5 credits in SSCE/GCE O'Level including English Language and Mathematics
  • JAMB UTME score of at least 120
  • Age requirement: Not less than 16 years
  • Post-UTME screening examination

Higher National Diploma (HND)

  • Relevant ND with minimum of Lower Credit
  • One year post-ND industrial experience (SIWES)
  • Relevant O'Level requirements
  • HND screening examination
